# OctoAcme Project Management Docs (README)

This README collects the OctoAcme project management process documents and provides a short summary of the processes used across the project to help team members find and follow established practices.

## Project Management Processes Summary

OctoAcme follows a structured project management approach across seven key process areas:

### Initiation
Project intake, objectives, and success criteria are defined during the project initiation phase. This ensures alignment on what we're building, why, and what success looks like.

### Planning
Scope, milestones, resourcing, and scheduling guidance help teams plan their work effectively. This includes capacity planning and dependency mapping.

### Execution & Tracking
Work is executed according to plan, with regular progress tracking and status updates. Team members follow established workflows and communication practices to keep stakeholders informed.

### Risks & Communication
Risk identification and mitigation strategies are documented, escalation paths are defined, and stakeholder communication practices ensure transparency and timely information flow.

### Release & Deployment
A structured approach to releases includes checklists, deployment steps, and rollback guidance to ensure safe and reliable deployments.

### Retrospective & Continuous Improvement
Regular retrospectives are held to reflect on what worked well and what could be improved. Action items are tracked to drive continuous improvement.

### Roles & Personas
Core roles and responsibilities are documented, along with contact points and escalation paths for different situations.
